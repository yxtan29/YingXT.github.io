Napster

By Ying Tan
2018 · 1 Post, 13 min

Napster began as a peer-to-peer (P2P) file sharing network that focuses on sharing digital audio files in MP3 formats. The platform allowed users to share their MP3 files with other participants within that same network. Many music fans recall the sense of liberation when Napster was launched, marking as the day music was set free. The service was developed by two teenagers in 1999, co-founders Shawn Fanning and Sean Parker, revolutionizing the way in which we share music. Read More
